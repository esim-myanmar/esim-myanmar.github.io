Device Verification Guide

Follow these steps to verify eSIM compatibility on your device

01

Check Device Settings

Navigate to your device settings and look for "Cellular" or "Mobile Network" options

02

Locate eSIM Option

Look for "Add Cellular Plan" or "Add Mobile Plan" in your network settings

03

Verify EID Number

Dial *#06# to display your device's EID (Embedded Identity Document)

04

Contact Support

If unsure, contact our technical support team for personalized assistance
